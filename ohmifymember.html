I'm Oyvind - I created Ohmify!

I fell in love with electronics when I was just 14.

I wanted to create circuits, to make things that actually work.

Learning from scratch was a struggle at first though. I had to gather learning resources from so many different places.

And so many of them went into way too much detail about components, circuits, concepts – using all sorts of complex words and ideas. I just wanted to make things!

But I loved creating working gadgets.

So I kept with it. I loved the feeling of designing a circuit, selecting the components, putting it together, and then finding that it actually worked.

My schoolboy passion for electronics got me to Oslo University – and that turned into a Master’s Degree in Electronics.

I’m a published author now – and have been involved in some pretty heavyweight industrial electronics projects – but I have never lost sight of the fact that, at its heart, practical electronics is straightforward, uncomplicated, and great fun to learn.

I still love building things and I really do love helping people find out just how much fun all this is!

So I created Ohmify for people who really just want to get on with creating electronics.

Your Questions Answered

I'm a pensioner/retired and I haven't done any electronics in the last 50 years. Would I be able to follow along the courses?

Yes, absolutely. We have members from 10 to over 80 years old with a wide variety of different backgrounds. You do not need any previous knowledge to join. And you decide how quick or slow you want to go through the courses.

Can I try it for one month only?

Yes, absolutely.

Just choose the ‘Monthly’ option to start a monthly subscription. Then make sure you cancel the subscription from your account settings before the next month’s billing cycle starts (you’ll still have access until the end of the current period).

Do you offer a free trial?

We don’t. But we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you sign up and realize it was not what you wanted, just let us know within 30 days and you’ll get a full refund.

What's included with a membership?

It includes:

  • Access to all the courses in the course library (and new ones are developed continuously)
  • Access to a community forum with both experts and beginners where you can get help with anything electronics-related
  • A beginner’s component kit (Free if you join on the yearly plan. Half price on the monthly plan)

…and other resources such as calculators and more.

I'm a musician with no previous experience in electronics. Will Ohmify work for me?

Many of our members are interested in music-related circuits such as guitar pedals, synthesizers, and amplifiers.

The key to building these is to understand circuit diagrams and knowing how to build circuits (from simple to more complicated).

And the key to understanding these circuits is to have a good understanding of the fundamentals and how the different basic components in electronics work. Then from there study the individual circuits you are interested in.

These are all things you’ll learn from the courses in Ohmify. And for things that you can’t find the answer to in the courses, there is an active forum with friendly people where you can ask questions or get help with your own projects.

Could the starter kit be purchased separately?

Yes, members can purchase the kit separately. You’ll also have access to the list of components so that you can buy them yourself from your local store if you prefer.

Is Ohmify kid-friendly?

Yes, Ohmify is kid-friendly. We especially recommend the Arduino courses as kids tend to enjoy those types of projects a lot.

Will I learn to repair electronics?

Yes. In the Ohmify library, you’ll have access to the course How To Repair Electronics.

And as you go through some of the other courses on basic electronics (also in the library), you’ll build a good understanding of electronics so that you’ll become even better at finding circuit problems and repairing them.

I can build circuits on a breadboard but have never made a PCB. If I sign up will I get guidance on going from breadboard to a permanent circuit?

Oh yes, definitely =)

The breadboard is just to get started. But I encourage everyone to eventually design their own printed circuit boards (PCB).

We’ve run live workshops on this. And there are currently two courses on how to design PCBs in the library that you’ll get access to. One very basic to get your first board up and running – and one more advanced where you design a kitchen timer using a microcontroller.

In addition to those courses, we always have your back if you get stuck or have any questions during the process.

Can the courses help me learn to build a movie prop? Like a Star Wars lightsaber?

Yes, absolutely. We don’t have a “how to build a lightsaber” project in the library (yet), but you’ll learn all the skills you would need to put one together. And you’d have the support of the community forum to help build whatever you want to build.

Is it possible to buy just one course?

Well, no. But you could sign up for one month, go through just the course you want, then cancel. When we sold single courses a few years back, the courses were much more expensive than the current monthly fee.

Does Ohmify work for homeschooling?

Yes, we have several people using Ohmify for homeschooling. We offer a Roadmap that tells you what course to start with, where to go next, etc. And it’s also possible to ask for advice on how to organize the courses if you have any special requests.

Will I be tied to a long-term contract?

No, we don’t force you into any long-term contracts. Our best price is available as an annual subscription, but we offer monthly subscriptions as well. Just cancel before the next renewal if you don’t want to continue.

Where do you send kits from?

Our warehouse is located in the United States. All kits are sent from this location.
