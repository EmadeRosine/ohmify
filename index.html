LESSONS

What do you learn?

Basic Electronics

Learning electronics doesn’t have to be hard – take our classes to master the basics and everything else falls into place

Basic Components

How do the basic electronics components work? Build simple circuits to see for yourself and enforce your learning

Soldering

Soldering is a fundamental skill – it’s fun and it’s easy. In Ohmify you’ll see exactly how to do it – both basic and more advanced soldering

Arduino

Learn microcontroller programming with Arduino so that you can add WiFi, Bluetooth, displays and more to your projects

Printed circuit boards

Learn to create your own printed circuit boards. The ultimate circuit-building skill that makes complex circuits easy to build!

circuit diagrams

Learn how to read circuit diagrams and understand how different devices work. Then design your own!

CHIPS (OR INTEGRATED CIRCUITS)

A chip, or an integrated circuit can be anything from a music player circuit to a remote control circuit. Learn how to use them in your circuits

No previous knowledge necessary

Many of the projects we work on are pre-designed. So you get step-by-step instructions that build the gadget from scratch to completion. No previous knowledge is assumed.

Other projects… well, if you can dream it up then you can usually make it! You’ll quickly gain the knowledge to create your own projects. But if you get stuck I or the community helps you out. So you never stay stuck.

You don’t have to wonder how electronics works. You can know how electronics works!
